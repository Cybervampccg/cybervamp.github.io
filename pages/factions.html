The Four Eternal Factions

Four bloodlines, four paths to power in the Crimson Nexus.

Red – Neon Revenants

Young, willingly transformed vampires obsessed with speed, technology, and pushing limits. High-risk, high-reward playstyle in glowing megacities.

Leader: Zane "Z" Voss – visionary self-turned hacker, Elon Musk archetype.

Founding: Zane viral-marketed synth-bite tech; the Neon Ignition (2155) rallied zoomers in a hacker-revolution.

Signature Traits

  • Haste (highest density)
  • Bleed (high)
  • Self-Bleed (exclusive to Red)
  • Overclock (+power starting T4+)

Playstyle

Reckless aggro: Fast, explosive boards; self-damage tax creates tension but synergies (purge own Bleed, redirect) turn it into advantage.

Power Curve

Slightly ahead of curve starting T3–4.

Weakness

Fragile to wipes; self-damage can backfire without clears.

White – Eternal Aristocracy

Ancient vampires turned by old methods, hoarding vast wealth and relics in baroque crystal palaces.

Leader: Count Vladislav Dracul – classic Dracula archetype, regal and calculating.

Founding: Vladislav rallied noble houses post-war, orchestrated the Crystal Ascension (2152) to unite aristocrats and formalize human stewardship.

Signature Traits

  • Wall (high power, no combat damage)
  • Life Gain / Bleed removal (on block/relics)
  • Relic synergy & recursion
  • Overclock (late-game)

Playstyle

Legacy control/ramp: Stall with walls + life gain, ramp via draw/scry + reusable relics, overwhelm late with massive bombs.

Power Curve

Underpowered attackers early, massive bombs T5+.

Weakness

Slow start; vulnerable to fast aggro if walls are removed.

Purple – Arcane Scholars

Mystic knowledge-seekers on floating isles and ancient universities. Avoid direct conflict, manipulate through clairvoyance and psychic power.

Leader: Archsage Alaric Voss – wise mentor, Dumbledore archetype.

Founding: Alaric predicted war outcomes, founded floating isles; the Aether Awakening (2153) synod unveiled mystic algorithms.

Signature Traits

  • Subterfuge (tap opponent creatures)
  • Direct damage spells
  • Exhaust/overexhaust hate (destroy overexhausted targets)

Playstyle

Heavy control/disruption: Tap, direct pings, exhaust punishment; heavy draw/scry; win via incremental damage or lockouts.

Weakness

Lower board presence; struggles vs wide aggro if disruption is out-tempoed.

Black – Shadow Behemoths

Primal ritualists in catacombs and swampy bayous. Command beasts, siphon life, reanimate the fallen.

Leader: Dr. Elias Crowe, the Fleshweaver – mad scientist, Frankenstein archetype.

Founding: Elias fused with his necro-experiments in bunkers; formed the Necro-Pact (2150) binding feral vampires and tribes.

Signature Traits

  • Siphon (gain life = damage/Bleed dealt)
  • Reanimate (play from discard, enters renewed)
  • Minion Summon (weak beast/bat/wolf tokens)

Playstyle

Necromantic grinder: Mid-game swarm with minions, siphon to outlast, reanimate threats, deaden opponent resources (turn colored Blood colorless).

Weakness

Vulnerable to exile effects on discard pile; minions are fragile.
