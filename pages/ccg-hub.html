Rules Codex

The Will manifests through blood, gold, and technae. Learn the laws of the Crimson Nexus.

Game Overview & Core Theme

A collectible card game set in a post-Eclipse War world of four vampire factions (Red, White, Black, Purple). Each faction has distinct colors, mechanics, and flavor. Players represent one faction and build mono-color or light-splash decks.

Setup

  • Starting Life: Both players begin with 20 Nexus Vitality (life).
  • Decks (60 cards total):
    • Main Deck: 40 cards (creatures, spells, relics)
    • Land Deck: 20 cards (only lands)
  • Starting Hand: Draw 7 cards (any mix from main + land decks).
  • Mulligan: If <2 or >4 lands, redraw 7 (shuffle back).

Turn Structure

  1. Untap: Untap all exhausted/overexhausted cards.
  2. Draw: Draw 1 card — choose from Main Deck or Land Deck.
  3. Pre-Battle (Main Phase): Play 1 land per turn. Cast spells, creatures, relics. Bleed counters trigger here (damage = counters, then remove).
  4. Battle: Declare attackers → blockers → resolve combat.
  5. Post-Battle: End-of-turn effects, cleanup.

Resources

Blood (colored mana: Red, White, Black, Purple) — powers spells.

Gold (colorless) — required (along with Blood) for creatures and relics.

Lands (build your 20-card Land Deck)

  • Faction Blood Lands (Red/White/Black/Purple): Produce 1 colored Blood.
  • Gold Land: Produce 1 Gold.
  • Ultra-rare Nexus Flux Land: Produce 1 any Blood or 1 Gold, but deal 1 damage to controller.

Exhaust Mechanics

  • Exhaust (90° sideways): Standard tap for resources/abilities; untaps next turn.
  • Overexhaust (180° flipped, special trait only):
    • Activate from upright state.
    • Doubles output or adds bonus effect.
    • Takes 2 full untap phases to renew (vulnerable window).

Combat

Creatures have Power only (no toughness). Summoning sickness applies unless Haste. Attacking exhausts (unless Vigilance).

Resolution (simultaneous):

  • Unblocked: Deals Power to opponent life.
  • Blocked: Both deal Power to each other. Higher destroys lower; excess Tramples if attacker has Trample. Equal: Both exhaust (no destroy).

Bleed (X)

On direct player damage (unblocked or trample excess): Opponent gains X Bleed counters.

At end of damaged player's Pre-Battle: Take damage = counters, then remove all Bleed counters.

Factions

Red – Neon Revenants

Reckless cyber-zoomer aggro. High-risk/high-reward.

  • Traits: Haste (highest), Bleed (high), Self-Bleed (exclusive), Overclock (T4+ power).
  • Playstyle: Explosive boards, self-damage synergies (purge/redirect).
  • Power curve: Ahead T3–4.
  • Weakness: Fragile to wipes, self-damage backfire.

White – Eternal Aristocracy

Ancient legacy control/ramp. Patient, defensive.

  • Traits: Wall (high power, no damage out), Life Gain/Bleed removal, Relic recursion, late Overclock.
  • Playstyle: Stall + ramp relics, late bombs.
  • Power curve: Slow early, massive T5+.
  • Weakness: Vulnerable to fast aggro without walls.

Purple – Arcane Scholars

Mystic subterfuge control. Manipulation-focused.

  • Traits: Subterfuge (tap foes), Direct damage, Exhaust/overexhaust hate.
  • Playstyle: Disruption, draw/scry, incremental wins/lockouts.
  • Weakness: Low board presence, struggles vs wide aggro.

Black – Shadow Behemoths

Primal necromantic grinder. Swarm & outlast.

  • Traits: Siphon (life = damage/Bleed), Reanimate (from discard, renewed), Minion tokens.
  • Playstyle: Mid-game grind, siphon outlast, resource deaden.
  • Weakness: Exile hurts discard, fragile minions.
