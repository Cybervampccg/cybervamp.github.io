Metaphysical Core

Technae & The Blood Covenant

Technae is the shimmering crimson energy field that vampires naturally attune to post-turning ‚Äî a fusion of vampiric vitae and quantum nanotechnology. It amplifies strength to god-tier, speed to Mach velocities, and interfaces with tech and magic on an intuitive level.

Technae draws from blood mana. Overuse causes Vitae Burnout ‚Äî feral rages and tech-glitches until replenished. This tension is the soul of gameplay.

"The Veiled Accord binds us to humanity ‚Äî not as masters, but as protectors. They give us blood. We give them eternity. The Nexus Accord is etched in technae-blockchain. Break it, and face sunlight vulnerability. Forever."‚Äî The Crimson Council, Founding Charter

Blood Harvest is automated via Vitae Courier Drones ‚Äî faction-specific craft delivering painless kits, collecting, scanning, returning. Tapping a land is a Resonance Harvest: harmonious, non-destructive. Humans live in fortified village Land Nodes, receiving longevity treatments, neural upgrades, and security in exchange for their donations.

Turn Structure

The Eternal Night Cycle

Each turn mirrors the eternal rhythm of the hunt. Master it, and your enemies will never see the killing blow coming.

I
Renew
Untap all your permanents. Exhausted Land Nodes regenerate. Overexhausted cards advance one step toward Recoalescence.
"The eternal night resets. Blood flows back into drained veins. Vitae-drones begin their morning rounds."
II
Draw
Draw 1 card ‚Äî your choice of Main Deck or Land Deck. A strategic decision made every single turn.
"The Will of the Nexus infuses fresh directives ‚Äî another summoned entity, another relic uncovered from the archives."
III
Pre-Battle
Play 1 Land Node. Deploy creatures and relics. Activate abilities. Build your court for the coming Veil Clash.
"Sire Lords assemble their forces ‚Äî thralls take position, relics activate, village gates draw shut."
IV
Battle ‚Äî The Veil Clash
Declare attackers ‚Üí opponent declares blockers ‚Üí damage resolves simultaneously. Unblocked creatures trigger Bleed. Breach lets excess damage pierce through to the player's Vitae Core. Destruction is banishment to shadows, not true death.
"Veil Clashes are ritual duels where power symbolizes dominance. The loser is scattered ‚Äî not destroyed, but banished until recalled."
V
Post-Battle
Deploy more permanents, trigger end-of-turn effects. Bleed counters erupt ‚Äî lose life equal to your total Bleed, then all counters are removed. Clean up and pass the night.
"The blood debt settles. Vitae Curses fester and discharge. The Nexus exhales. Another cycle begins."
